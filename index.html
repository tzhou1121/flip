👑 How to Wear an Invisible Crown

👑 Step 1: Be Silent.
Power doesn’t speak. It waits. The louder you are, the less they listen. Breathe slowly. Maintain eye contact. Say nothing. Let them assume you know everything.

👑 Step 2: Encode Everything.
Symbols are your shield. QR codes are your sword. Obscurity is currency. Say “temporalities” and you’re untouchable.

👑 Step 3: Glaze It.
The shinier, the deeper (they’ll believe). High-gloss reflects not your work, but their projection. Nobody argues with a mirror.

👑 Step 4: Cite Foucault.
Even if it’s about noodles. Especially if it’s about noodles.

👑 Step 5: Cite Žižek Too.
Mispronounce it slightly. Reference “the Real.” Throw in toilets or Hitchcock. Confusion is your camouflage.

👑 Step 6: Do Absolutely Nothing.
Stillness is elite behavior. Inertia reads as intellect. Disappear and let them write your myth.

👑 Bonus Tip: Never explain. They’ll call it conceptual.
👑 Corollary: If they don’t understand it, they’ll fund it.
